Interesting Facts About Camels

1.Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.

3.Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

1.Their Feet Work Like Salad Tongs.

2.Chameleons vary widely in terms of size.

3.Smaller chameleons have faster tongues

Interesting Facts About Cicada

1.Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes

2.The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus. (Females make a much quieter sound by flicking their wings.)

3They improve lawns by digging tunnels that allow air into the soil. When they die, their rotting bodies put nitrogen into the soil.

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

1.Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals. They are one of only a small number of species to pass the ‘mirror test’ – a test of self recognition.

2.Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph. The fastest recorded speed is 92.5 mph.

3.Despite the social perception as dirty and disease-ridden, pigeons are actually very clean animals and there is very little evidence to suggest that they are significant transmitters of disease.
